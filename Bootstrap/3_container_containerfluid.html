1. Chhota Bheem and the Stolen Laddoos One morning in Dholakpur, Bheem woke up excited to eat his favorite laddoos. But when he reached the kitchen, all the laddoos were missing! The villagers were worried because the King’s feast was that evening. Bheem, Chutki, and Jaggu started searching the village. They followed sweet crumbs that led them to Kalia, who had hidden the laddoos to take credit for saving the day. Bheem calmly talked to Kalia and explained that teamwork is more important than showing off. Feeling ashamed, Kalia returned the laddoos. Everyone praised Bheem for his kindness, and the feast was a grand success.

2. Doraemon’s Magical Pencil Nobita was scared because he had a drawing competition at school. Doraemon gave him a magical pencil that could bring drawings to life. Nobita drew a beautiful bird, and it flew around the room! Soon, Nobita started misusing the pencil, creating shortcuts for everything. But the drawings began causing trouble. Doraemon explained that magic cannot replace hard work. Nobita apologized and returned the pencil. He practiced sincerely and won second prize, learning a valuable lesson about effort and honesty

3. Tom and Jerry: The Cheese Festival Tom planned to catch Jerry during the annual cheese festival. He set traps everywhere, but Jerry cleverly avoided them. Suddenly, a bigger cat tried to steal all the cheese. Realizing the danger, Tom and Jerry teamed up to protect the festival. Together, they chased the intruder away. In the end, Jerry shared some cheese with Tom. For one day, they forgot their rivalry and enjoyed the celebration together.

4. Shinchan and the School Picnic Shinchan was super excited about his school picnic. On the way, he played pranks on his friends, making everyone laugh. But when Kazama got lost in the forest, Shinchan became serious. He remembered the path and helped the teachers find Kazama safely. Everyone was surprised by Shinchan’s responsibility. The picnic ended happily, and Shinchan learned that fun is best when mixed with care for others.
